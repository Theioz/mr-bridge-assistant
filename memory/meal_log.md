# Meal Log

## Cuisine Preferences
- Primary: Korean, Southeast Asian
- Style: Adventurous eater, comfortable with high-quality proteins and seafood

## Core Cooking Principles
- Dry brine proteins ahead of time when possible
- Temperature > time — use thermometer
- Rest all meats before slicing
- High heat for searing, low heat for control
- Add fats (butter/oil) late to prevent burning

## Staple Ingredients
- Olive oil
- Garlic
- Gochujang
- Old Bay
- Lemon
- Butter
- Ginger
- Marinara
- Chicken broth
- Cream cheese
- Brown rice / pasta

## Recipe Library

### Meal Prep

#### Ribeye Roast Meal Prep
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Servings | 6–8 |
| Category | Meal Prep |

**Ingredients:**
- 3 lb ribeye roast
- 1 tbsp salt, 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp black pepper
- 2 bunches broccolini, 2 zucchini, 1.5 tbsp olive oil, salt + pepper

**Method:**
1. Dry brine overnight
2. Roast at 275°F until 130°F internal (~75–90 min)
3. Rest 20 min
4. Roast vegetables at 425°F for 20 min
5. Slice thin and portion

#### Lemon Garlic Chicken + Pasta
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Servings | 10–11 |
| Category | Meal Prep |

**Ingredients:**
- 12 drumsticks, 11 wings
- 3 tbsp olive oil, 1 lemon (juice + zest), garlic, oregano, thyme
- Pasta, corn, spinach, green beans
- Chicken broth + cream cheese

**Method:**
1. Marinate 12–24 hrs
2. Bake drumsticks 400°F (35–45 min)
3. Air fry wings 375°F (20–25 min)
4. Build sauce with broth + cream cheese
5. Combine with pasta + vegetables

#### Chicken, Rice, Green Beans
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Servings | 5 |
| Category | Meal Prep |

**Ingredients:**
- 3 lb chicken breast, 2 cups brown rice
- Green beans, olive oil + lemon

**Method:**

### Steaks / High Quality

#### Reverse-Seared Ribeye
**Method:**
1. Oven 250°F until 95°F internal
2. Rest 10 min
3. Sear 1 min per side
4. Baste with butter
5. Rest 5 min

---

#### Wagyu Ribeye + Lobster + Potatoes
**Method (order matters):**
1. Boil potatoes, then roast at 425°F
2. Slow cook steak
3. Bake lobster 400°F (8–10 min)
4. Sear steak last
